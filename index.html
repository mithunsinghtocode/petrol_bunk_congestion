Best Times to Refuel

  • Early morning (5:00 — 7:00 AM) before rush hour
  • Late night (after 10:00 PM) when queues thin out
  • Mid-week days (Tue–Thu) tend to be less crowded
  • Avoid weekends and public holidays

Fuel Conservation Tips

  • Maintain proper tire pressure — saves up to 3% fuel
  • Avoid aggressive acceleration and braking
  • Turn off engine if idling for more than 30 seconds
  • Remove unnecessary weight from your vehicle
  • Use AC sparingly — open windows at low speeds
  • Plan trips to combine multiple errands
  • Consider carpooling with neighbors

During Rationing

  • Check if odd/even plate number rules apply in your area
  • Carry vehicle registration and ID at all times
  • Know your daily/weekly purchase limits
  • Essential services (ambulance, fire) get priority

Alternative Transport

  • Use public transport when possible
  • Consider cycling for short distances
  • Work from home if your employer allows
  • Explore EV charging stations if you have an electric vehicle
